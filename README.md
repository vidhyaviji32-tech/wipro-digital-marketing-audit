# Comprehensive Audit and Analysis for Effective Digital Presence - Wipro Limited

## Project Overview
A comprehensive digital presence audit for Wipro Limited covering website audit, digital marketing strategy analysis, and a newly created SEO friendly landing page.

## About Wipro
- Full Name: Wipro Limited
- Founded: 1945
- Headquarters: Bangalore, India
- Employees: 250,000 plus
- Operations: 65 plus countries
- Revenue: 11.3 Billion USD in 2024
- Services: Cloud, AI, Cybersecurity, IT Consulting
- Clients: 1000 plus global clients

## Website Audit Report

### Tools Used
PageSpeed Insights, RankMath SEO Analyzer, Responsive Design Checker

### Findings
- Page Speed: Good on Desktop, needs improvement on Mobile
- SEO Score: Meta tags present, keywords optimized
- Design: Clean, professional and user friendly
- Mobile Responsive: Yes
- SSL Certificate: Present
- Navigation: Easy and intuitive

## Digital Marketing Strategy Analysis

### Current Traditional Marketing
TV and print advertisements, industry conferences and events, sponsorships and billboards

### Current Digital Marketing
SEO and content marketing, LinkedIn ads and posts, email marketing, YouTube and webinars, Google Ads

### Recommendations
- Instagram Reels and short videos
- Regional language content
- Tech influencer collaborations
- AI chatbot customer engagement
- Focus digital marketing for lead generation
- Use traditional marketing for brand awareness

## Landing Page Created

### Tool Used
WordPress and Elementor

### Sections Created
Hero Section, About Us, Our Services covering Cloud AI Cybersecurity and IT Consulting, Why Choose Us, Client Testimonials, Contact Section, Footer

### SEO Optimization Done
- Focus Keyword: Wipro IT Services
- Meta Title and Description added
- RankMath SEO Plugin used
